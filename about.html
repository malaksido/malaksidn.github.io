Discover Algeria

Algeria

is a great country for the richness of its history and culture that can be discovered by visiting the different Algerian towns. The Algerian population is very warm and hospitable. Algeria also has important natural assets. A land with impressive contrasts. From the Numides to the Arab through the Phoenicians, Romans, Vandals and Byzantines, Algeria has always attracted desires. Algeria tends to make you discover a long forgotten heritage.
